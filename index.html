Welcome to ADDO!

AADO is a women Led and an indigenous non - governmental organization legally registered in South Sudan with registration #2604. AADO was stablished in 2018 by volunteer professionals in South Sudan as a private, voluntary, nonpolitical, and nonsectarian organization.

AADO national office is situated in Juba Na Bari next to Bilpam Road behind Shabele Petrol stations former IGAD offices
